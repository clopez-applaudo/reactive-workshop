Lab 3 — RxJS ↔ Signals Interop

Goal: bridge the two reactive worlds cleanly: toSignal() and toObservable().

Mental model

Observable → Signal: toSignal(obs$) gives you a synchronous read in templates (signal()). Signal → Observable: toObservable(sig) lets you use RxJS operators like debounce/switchMap. Best practice: keep complex async workflows in RxJS, expose results to templates as Signals.
